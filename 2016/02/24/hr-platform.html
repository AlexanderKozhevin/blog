HR-platform

HR - platform is a quite simple tool to organize & manage contacts, job applications and events. The idea was suggested from a friend of mine back in 2014. But only 1.5 years later it was completed. We just delayed a thing. But 6 months ago when I quit a regular job I decided to take matters into own hands. The development process itself wasn't really straight. I played with technologies and libraries. When I got the first version of it I decided to rewrite it completely. Cause it wasn't really friendly. But finally in two months I made it about the same I wanted it.
And there are few interesting things I want to share with you.
Interface in 1 Minute
Interface itself is based on material design. I guess, it's a great solution for small projects and startups. Cause minus one huge headache is taken away.

Angular Material is used within this project. It has number of primary elements and very probably it's more than enough. Generally it feels like a medium quality lego-constructor. Excellent for small apps, so-so for average and difficult for large ones.
